HXLLOGHXST on BandCamp

Back in 2023, before Auralithic Collective was ever born, Sun Dream and HXLLOGHXST collaborated to host a collection of beats, using Sun Dream's bandcamp page. Technically, this could be considered the first release from HXLLOGHXST. Future releases would then on be put out via Auralithic Collective through Spotify, Soundcloud and Youtube.
