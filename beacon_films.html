Beacon Films

Beacon Films has been on youtube under many names before, unfortunately those channels are lost to time - forgotten and deleted. This time, he’s not letting anyone tell him different. Beacon Films is part vlog - part storytelling. We learn more about the mindset of Cristian Barraza; who he is, what he does and how he thinks.

One video that encapsulates his channel is the Volvo 240 movie. You can go back into his channel and see a video that showcases how fun the car is for him and why he loves it, despite its flaws; a brilliant juxtaposition against the ad he made to sell the car. He made the video to accompany a facebook marketplace ad; not being satisfied with only 10 pictures to sell what was his favorite car. Even comments on reddit were confused, asking “This is a lot of effort to advertise a sell for a car that you claim was your favorite. Why?”. It was a love letter to send off the vehicle in style. Not just doomed to be forgotten in a marketplace ad once it sold.

We follow Cristian in his problem solving ventures, some made up, others necessary. It’s a vital part of his work to have a message behind what he does. Vlogging daily life isn’t interesting if nothing is going on, so finding a reason to do something is what fuels his movies. As a peek into his history making movies, he compiled a playlist of Video Flyers he made for Sister Fisters, a drag talent agency in Austin, TX. This is where he birthed his VHS-like style for editing. Valentine Quarantine is his favorite, with A Tribute to The Strokes | This is It being a close second. Keen-eyed fans will recognize that he is the lead singer of the now disbarred Strokes tribute band.

Cristian Barraza is a Visual Thinker, meaning he thinks using pictures, rotating cubes and putting images to words. “How can I make my vision come to life?” In his 3-part movie Repairs, Bags and Pink Crocs, we see his encounter with a backyard gate that isn’t broken, but could use fixing. Jay Jones’ Desk Stand goes deeper into visualizing his thought process, showing the diagrams that eventually birth a physical desk stand, a gift for his best friend. When he goes on lectures like in Intermediate Fortnite Tips and Tricks or A Cinematic Move about Tape, he always accompanies the narration with a visual of the problem that needs solving and how to fix it.

Beacon Films on Youtube

Cristian has always made movies from a young age, using whatever camera was available.

This channel is made to tell the day-to-day stories Cristian goes through.

Beacon Films posts every friday on Youtube at 9:00 AM CST and won’t stop until there’s nothing left to say.


Beacon Films for Sister Fisters

In 2018, Sister Fisters reached out to Beacon Films to create video flyers for drag show events in Austin, TX.

This is where the VHS-like aesthetic was born, embracing mistakes, imperfections and natural degradation.
