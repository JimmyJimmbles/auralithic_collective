Auralithic Collective Artists

No matter what Art you make, Auralithic Collective is behind you. At Auralithic Collective, we believe in the power of being entirely yourself. Authenticity is a core facet of being an artist, so no hesitation will be tolerated. In order to become great, you must be willing to do something you’ve never done before - which we entirely support. Austin, TX is the music capital of the world, where the motto here is “Keep Austin Weird”.

At Auralithic Collective, we not only cater to the weirdos, the freaks, the uniques and the artists, but seek to highlight them. Give us your most experimental, underground and fun people and we’ll make something truly special.

Self-Expression is paramount. Auralithic Collective is born from appreciating the underground; the diamonds in the rough, the artists unapologetically themselves.
